Technology the new way of doing things


Set up as a 100% subsidiary of Rift Valley Corporation in 2009, Rift Valley Energy (RVE) develops, owns and operates renewable energy projects in sub-Saharan Africa. Over the last few years, RVE’s public-private-partnership approach has resulted in a meaningful project development track record, including 4.6 MW of operational generating assets in Tanzania and Zimbabwe, and a 4 MW hydro generation and rural electrification project in Tanzania. RVE sells to commercial customers, rural communities and the national grid.
